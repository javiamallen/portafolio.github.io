1. The Challenge (LinkedIn Learning)

The core task was to utilize AI to generate a professional presentation outline. This required precise instruction design to meet specific content and format constraints.

Challenge Requirements:

  • **Objective:** Create a presentation outline/slides.
  • **Topic:** Prompt Engineering.
  • **Audience:** From your industry/discipline (Tech Consultants).

Output Constraints:

  • **Format:** ~6 slides.
  • **Content:** ~5 key points per slide.
  • **Length:** ~6-8 words per point.

2. The Engineered Solution: Structured Prompting

The solution involved crafting a structured prompt that treated the AI as a predictable API, ensuring the output adhered to all challenge constraints. This goes beyond simple conversational AI.

Structured Prompt (YAML Configuration Style)
# Defines a structured data approach to prompting
---
role: "Senior Technical Advisor, Generative AI Specialist"
task: "Generate a 6-slide presentation outline."
topic: "Prompt Engineering for Business Value"

audience:
  type: "Technology Consultants"
  needs: "Business applications, not just academic theory."

constraints:
  - format: "6 slides"
  - content: "5 key points per slide"
  - length: "~6-8 words per point"
  - tone: "Professional, informative, direct"
---

This YAML-style approach demonstrates how I structure data for an AI, emphasizing **Role-Based Tasking**, **Audience Scoping**, and **Strict Output Constraints** to achieve precise results.
